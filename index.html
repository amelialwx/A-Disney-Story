what is your color?

Hover over a color in the middle ring to see what it represents! You can also hover over the inner ring to learn more about each color group. The outer ring represents whether that character is good, neutral, or bad.

Let's learn about the Disney Universe!

Hover over each dot to see the movie name and the produced year! You can spot our movies with Disney princesses by hovering over their pictures.

where are you most loved?

Find out which princess is each country's favorite by hovering over the map!

Let's meet your friends (& foes)!

Take a look at the relationships that our princesses have! Pink Mickeys are our princesses. Hover over each Mickey to see who each character is connected to and how strong their relationship score are! The thicker the line between two Mickeys, the stronger the connection between those two characters.

Connections

A good princess can bring success to the movie.

Does a princess affect the performance of the movie? The answer is YES! A better rated princess would increase the revenue of the entire franchise. Hover over the bubbles to see how you and other princesses did!
